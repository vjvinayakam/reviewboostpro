FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a WhatsApp Business API account?+
No! ReviewBoostPRO uses the standard wa.me link method. You click a button, WhatsApp opens with the message pre-filled, and you hit send from your own phone. No API needed.
Is this against Google's terms?+
Asking customers for reviews is fully allowed by Google. ReviewBoostPRO simply asks how their experience was — used by thousands of businesses globally.
How quickly will I see more reviews?+
Most businesses see new Google reviews within 24–48 hours. On average, a 3–5× increase in reviews within 60 days.
Is there a free trial?+
Yes — 14 days free with full access to Growth plan features. No credit card required.
