FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a WhatsApp Business API account?+
No! ReviewBoostPRO uses the standard wa.me link method. You click a button, WhatsApp opens with the message pre-filled, and you hit send from your own phone. No API, no approvals, no extra cost.
Is this against Google's terms of service?+
Asking customers for reviews is fully allowed by Google. What's not allowed is incentivising reviews (like offering discounts for 5-stars). ReviewBoostPRO simply asks how their experience was — this is used by thousands of businesses globally.
How quickly will I see more Google reviews?+
Most businesses see new Google reviews within 24–48 hours of their first campaign. On average, customers see a 3–5× increase in reviews within 60 days.
What happens to negative feedback?+
Customers who rate 1–3 stars are sent to a private feedback form instead of Google. That feedback goes to your dashboard inbox so you can resolve it quietly — before it becomes a public 1-star review.
Is there a free trial?+
Yes — 14 days free with full access to Growth plan features. No credit card required.
