FAQ

Questions from doctors

Do I need a WhatsApp Business account or any API approval?
+
No — ReviewBoostPRO works with your existing personal or clinic WhatsApp number. No API approvals, no WhatsApp Business setup, no additional fees. Just open WhatsApp as you normally do.
Is it ethical to send review requests to patients?
+
Yes, completely. Asking patients for honest feedback is standard practice worldwide. ReviewBoostPRO never incentivises reviews or asks patients to write only positive ones — it simply makes it easy for happy patients to share their genuine experience on Google.
What happens when a patient gives 1-3 stars?
+
They are directed to a private feedback form instead of Google. Their feedback comes directly to your dashboard inbox. You can address the concern privately. They never reach Google unless they choose to go there separately.
How many new reviews can I expect per month?
+
Most clinics sending 20–30 WhatsApp requests per week see 5–10 new Google reviews per month. WhatsApp has a 68% open rate vs 10% for email — far more patients respond. Even 5 new reviews per month is enough to significantly improve your Google Maps ranking over 2–3 months.
Can I send to multiple patients at once?
+
Yes — use the CSV bulk upload feature. Upload a file with patient names and numbers, and ReviewBoostPRO walks you through sending to each one. Perfect for sending to this week's patients every Friday.
Will this work for a small clinic with 10–15 patients a day?
+
Especially well. Small clinics benefit the most — going from 5 to 40 reviews has a dramatic impact on Google Maps ranking. Even sending to 5 patients per day generates enough reviews to reach the top 3 in most Indian cities within 60–90 days.
