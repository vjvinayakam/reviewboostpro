Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Effective immediately

📋 Quick Summary

✓
14-day free trial — no credit card required, no charge if you cancel before it ends
✓
7-day money-back guarantee on your first paid month — no questions asked
✓
Cancel anytime — access continues until end of billing period
✓
No refunds for months after the first paid month

1. Free Trial

All new ReviewBoostPRO accounts start with a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to start your trial.

If you cancel before the 14-day trial ends, you will not be charged anything. Your account simply stops at the end of the trial period.

2. Auto-Renewal

After your 14-day free trial ends, your subscription automatically renews at ₹499 per month. We will send you a reminder email before your trial ends so there are no surprises.

By continuing to use ReviewBoostPRO after the trial period, you agree to the monthly subscription charge.

3. Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on your first paid month. If you are not satisfied with ReviewBoostPRO for any reason, email us within 7 days of your first payment and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

⚠️ The 7-day money-back guarantee applies to your first paid month only. Subsequent monthly charges are non-refundable.

To request a refund, email support@reviewboost.in with your registered email address and the reason for your request. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

4. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • From your dashboard settings — go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel
  • By emailing support@reviewboost.in

When you cancel, your access continues until the end of your current billing period. We do not offer prorated refunds for cancellations mid-month after the first paid month.

5. What Is Not Refundable

  • Monthly charges after the first paid month
  • Partial months after cancellation
  • Charges where you forgot to cancel before the renewal date (we recommend setting a reminder)
  • Accounts suspended for violation of our Terms of Service

6. Payment Processing

Payments are processed securely via Razorpay. We do not store your card or banking details. All transactions are in Indian Rupees (₹).

If a payment fails, we will notify you by email and give you 3 days to update your payment method before your account is paused.

7. Exceptions

We review all refund requests on a case-by-case basis. In exceptional circumstances — such as a technical error on our part that prevented you from using the service — we may issue refunds beyond the standard policy at our discretion.

Have a question about your refund?

We respond to all refund requests within 24 hours on business days.
